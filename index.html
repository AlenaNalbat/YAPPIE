About

YUPPIE CLUB

  • What is YUPPIE?

    YUPPIE is a clothing and accessories brand founded in late 2023 in Kyiv.

    Together with you, we are recreating an authentic brand in the style of American Preppy, demonstrating that Ukrainians can be not only fans of this style, but also its creators. YUPPIE is not only about clothes, but also about life, work, sports and leisure, your hobbies, your personal victories, travel - in general, about you.

  • Uniqueness

    Preppy style

    There are many brands on the Ukrainian market that represent classic style, old money, but do not demonstrate the canonical American Preppy, because they do not rethink it to suit the needs of Ukrainians. We are not afraid to create unusual eclectic content, sometimes chaotic and experimental - just like the Preppy style. Our content is not trendy, minimalist or too pretentious, we are real, just like our brand, and we convey this through the atmosphere of our content.

  • What is Preppy?

    Preppy is NOT about minimalist monotonous looks in neutral shades

    Preppy is a style shaped by wealthy students, mainly from Ivy League universities, a rephrased classic style that has been given a young, sporty, daring character. It is a style that is easy to adapt to yourself, because it is open to any experiments and does not limit the manifestations of its authenticity.

  • The anchors of this style

    are high-quality natural fabrics, accent details, tennis skirts, polo shirts, bright ties, LETTERMAN clothing, etc

    A strong emphasis on unusual accessories and an unusual way of wearing them. Scarves, ties, bags, hats.
